* **Don‚Äôt use any VPN or proxy services** : such services could be blocked by NBA 2K‚Äôs servers, and therefore increase the chance of network problems like error code 727e66ac in NBA 2K24.
* **Reduce the number of devices connected to the same router** to ensure your main computer gets the most speed.

* **Play on your local server** . If that‚Äôs not an option, choose one that is closest to you.

* In your router settings, try to **enable QoS to prioritize gaming traffic** . If you‚Äôre not sure where to find it, please visit your router‚Äôs official website to find the manual or seek help from your ISP.

1. Press the **Windows logo key** and **R** on your keyboard to invoke the Run box.
