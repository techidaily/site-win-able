| **OS:**        | Windows 7 64-bit or newer                                                                |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Processor:** | Intel Pentium 4, Intel Core or AMD Athlon – 2.2 GHz +                                    |
| **Graphics:**  | Intel GMA X4500, NVIDIA GeForce 9600M GT or AMD/ATI Mobility Radeon HD 3650 – 256MB VRAM |
| **Memory:**    | 2 GB RAM                                                                                 |
