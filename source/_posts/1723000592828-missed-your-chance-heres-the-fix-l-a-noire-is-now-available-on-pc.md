**2)** Right-click**L.A. Noire** from the game list. Then, click**Properties** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/2-1.jpg)

**3)** Click the**Local Files** tab, and click**Verify Integrity of Game Files** .
